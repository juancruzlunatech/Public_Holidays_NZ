💡 Quick Information

🔄 What is Mondayisation?

Since 2014, Waitangi Day and Anzac Day are "Mondayised" — if they fall on a weekend, the following Monday becomes a day off work. The holidays are still commemorated on their actual dates.

🏪 Shop Closures

Most shops must be closed on Good Friday, Easter Sunday, Christmas Day, and the morning of Anzac Day (until 1pm). Some exemptions apply for small shops and essential services.

✨ Matariki

Matariki (Māori New Year) became a public holiday in 2022. It always falls on a Friday in June or July, based on the Māori lunar calendar and the rising of the Pleiades star cluster.

📍 Regional Holidays

Each region has its own anniversary day. If the date falls on a weekend, it's moved to a nearby Monday or Friday. Your regional holiday depends on where you work, not where you live.
