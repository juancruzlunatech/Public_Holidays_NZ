⭐ 2026 Highlights

🔄 Mondayisation in 2026

Anzac Day falls on Saturday 25 April — the observed day off is Monday 27 April. Boxing Day falls on Saturday 26 December — observed on Monday 28 December.

✨ Matariki 2026

Matariki falls on Friday, 10 July 2026. A great chance for a long winter weekend with whānau.

🐣 Easter 2026

Good Friday is on 3 April and Easter Monday on 6 April. A four-day weekend to recharge!
