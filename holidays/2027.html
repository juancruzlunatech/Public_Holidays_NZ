⭐ 2027 Highlights

🔄 Big year for Mondayisation

2027 is a big year for Mondayised holidays! Day after New Year's Day (Sat → Mon 4 Jan), Waitangi Day (Sat → Mon 8 Feb), Anzac Day (Sun → Mon 26 Apr), Christmas Day (Sat → Mon 27 Dec), and Boxing Day (Sun → Tue 28 Dec) are all transferred.

✨ Matariki 2027

Matariki falls on Friday, 25 June 2027. Enjoy a long weekend celebrating the Māori New Year with whānau.

🐣 Early Easter 2027

Good Friday is on 26 March and Easter Monday on 29 March. An early autumn break!
